HOW TO PLAY
Santase is a trick-taking game for two players with a 24-card deck. Win tricks, declare "twenty" and "forty" with King–Queen marriages, and be the first to reach 66 points. Close the talon at the right moment to press your advantage — but misjudge it and the round is lost. Simple to learn, deep to master.
